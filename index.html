About Me

I live in Wisconsin with my family and furbabies. I started my career in banking, but after some time, found myself at a crossroads looking for a new direction. Through some research and review of the job market, I had a decision to make. I could continue moving down the same path or discover something new and different. Different can be an exciting prospect, and that lead me to web development where I quickly fell in love with tech.

I've been involved in artistic endeavors my whole life, and the creative aspect of pulling a website together and making it come to life is fascinating. Once I started, I wanted to learn more. After building my first website, I found something that gave me a new view of the world that I had only been a spectator in prior. I have made the career switch to front-end web development and am using my organizational, problem resolution and detail oriented skills to write clean and elegant code.
